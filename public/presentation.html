RestoCMS

Restaurant Management Ecosystem

A complete digital solution for restaurants — manage your business and let customers find you online.

Two apps, one ecosystem

What is it?

RestoCMS is a two-part system:

🔐 RestoCMS (Admin)

Private dashboard for restaurant staff. Manage menu, reservations, tables, inventory, and settings.

🌐 RestoLine (Public)

Your restaurant's public website. Customers view the menu, book tables, and see business info.

Key Features

📋 Digital Menu

Update prices, photos, descriptions in real-time. Customers see changes instantly on RestoLine.

📅 Online Reservations

Customers book tables via the public site. You control slots, table counts, and opening hours.

📊 QR Code Menus

No app install. Customers scan a QR code at the table to view the menu and order.

🪑 Table Management

See which tables are occupied, reserved, or free at a glance.

📦 Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels for ingredients and supplies.

📧 Email Notifications

Automatic confirmations for reservations and orders.

🔍 SEO Tools

Control meta tags, descriptions, and search visibility.

🌍 Multi-language

English, French, and German out of the box.

How It Works

  1. Sign up — Create an account and name your restaurant.
  2. Set up your menu — Add categories, items, prices, and photos in the admin dashboard.
  3. Configure reservations — Set opening hours, table counts, and slot duration.
  4. Share your link — Customers visit RestoLine to see your menu and book tables.
  5. Manage daily — Use the dashboard to track orders, reservations, and inventory.
